# Thuiswerk

Educational tools for primary school children. Open the app in your browser — no install, no server, no account.

**[▶ Open Thuiswerk](https://mmalkus.github.io/Thuiswerk/)**

## Apps

### 🗺️ Topografie (Geography)
Learn to recognise Dutch provinces, cities and rivers, European countries and capitals, or physical geography features. Two modes: click the correct region on the map, or type the name of a highlighted feature. Fuzzy matching accepts minor spelling mistakes.

### ✏️ Dictee (Spelling)
Spelling practice with text-to-speech. Dutch (groep 3–8) and English (Year 1–6) word lists included. Tracks error history and uses weighted sampling to focus on difficult words.

### 🔢 Rekentoets (Maths Quiz)
Arithmetic quiz with configurable number ranges, operators (+, −, ×, ÷), and optional countdown timer. 20 questions per session with a score history chart.

## Install as app (PWA)

The site works as a Progressive Web App — install it for a full-screen, app-like experience with offline support.

### Android (Chrome)
1. Open [https://mmalkus.github.io/Thuiswerk/](https://mmalkus.github.io/Thuiswerk/) in Chrome.
2. Tap the **⋮** menu → **Add to Home screen**.
3. Tap **Add**. The app appears on your home screen and works offline.

### iPhone / iPad (Safari)
1. Open [https://mmalkus.github.io/Thuiswerk/](https://mmalkus.github.io/Thuiswerk/) in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow pointing up).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add**. The app opens full-screen from your home screen.

> Note: on iOS you must use Safari — Chrome and other browsers on iOS do not support PWA installation.

### Windows / Mac / Linux (Chrome or Edge)
1. Open [https://mmalkus.github.io/Thuiswerk/](https://mmalkus.github.io/Thuiswerk/) in Chrome or Edge.
2. Look for the **install icon** (⊕) in the address bar, or open the browser menu → **Install Thuiswerk…** / **Apps → Install this site as an app**.
3. Click **Install**. The app opens in its own window and appears in your taskbar / Dock / app launcher.
